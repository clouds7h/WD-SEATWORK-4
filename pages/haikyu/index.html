Hello, this is Kageyama

You might know me as the setter for Karasuno. Yeah, I used to be called the "King of the Court"—not exactly a compliment back then. I was all about winning, no matter what, and I didn't care how my teammates felt. But things changed. I learned that volleyball isn't just about me. It's about us. Trusting my teammates, working together, and really understanding each other on the court—that's what made us stronger. It's not just my journey; it's ours.

About Kageyama Tobio

Kageyama Tobio, also known as the "King of the Court," is one of the main characters in the popular sports anime Haikyu!!. He plays as a setter for Karasuno High School's volleyball team. Despite his initial arrogance and difficulty in working with others, Kageyama's exceptional skills and dedication to volleyball help him grow as both a player and a person. Throughout the series, he forms a powerful partnership with Hinata Shoyo, which becomes central to the team's dynamic and success.
